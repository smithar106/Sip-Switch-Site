Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

Sip Switch collects only the information necessary to provide personalised drink recommendations. This includes your taste preferences (quiz answers), drink ratings, and the moments you tell us about in the Live tab. We do not collect, store, or share sensitive personal data beyond what you choose to provide in the app.

How We Use Your Information

Your taste profile and ratings are used exclusively to improve your drink recommendations. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your preferences and ratings are stored locally on your device. No personal data is stored on external servers without your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

Sip Switch uses RevenueCat for subscription management. RevenueCat processes purchase-related data in accordance with their privacy policy. No taste profile or rating data is shared with RevenueCat.

Your Rights

You may delete your taste profile and all associated data at any time by removing the app from your device. For questions about your data, contact us at the support email listed on our Support page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@sipswitch.app.
